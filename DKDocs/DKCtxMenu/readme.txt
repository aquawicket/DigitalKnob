Context menu registry location map

These are the functions for many of the right click menu items available
[HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\CommandStore\shell]

     These are the HKLM paths related to right click menues
A  HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Classes\DesktopBackground\Shell
B  HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Classes\LibraryFolder\background\shell
C  HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Classes\Directory\background\shell
D  HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Classes\Directory\shell
E  HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Classes\Drive\shell
F  HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Classes\Folder\shell
G  HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Classes\*\shell

     These are the HKCR paths related to right click menues
H  HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\AllFileSystemObjects\Shell
I  HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\DesktopBackground\Shell
J  HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\LibraryFolder\background
K  HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\directory\background\shell
L  HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\directory\shell
M  HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\Drive\shell
N  HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\Folder\shell
O  HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\*\shell


You can basically put a menu item unere every one of these and you should be able
to cover most of the windows system.
